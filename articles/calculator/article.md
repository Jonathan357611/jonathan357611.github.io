# Calc-V1

## What is it?

![Calc-V1](./images/calc-v1.png)

Calc-V1 is a calculator using mechanical keyswitches based on a raspberry pi pico all soldered on
a custom PCB.
It's whole purpose is to annoy the people around me with these loud, clicky CherryMX blues :)
<br><br>

This was my very first PCB and I'm very happy how it came out,
but in hindsight I should have used different battery holders as the current ones
were very hard to order online and also kinda expensive.



<br><br><br>
## Parts used

If you want to get one yourself, you just need to order the .gerber files [here](https://github.com/Jonathan357611/Calc-V1) from any PCB-making service. Mine was made by JLCPCB and worked out fine.
<br><br>
Parts list:

- 19x keyswitches in the CherryMX form factor
- 1x Raspberry Pi Pico
- 1x breadboard switch
- 2x SSD1306 OLED screens
- 2x 2460 Keystone AA battery holders
